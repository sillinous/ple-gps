# Project Roadmap

This document describes where the PLE/GPS project is headed, what's actively in progress, and what's planned for future phases.

The roadmap is a living document — updated as priorities shift and working groups advance their work.

---

## North Star

Build the most rigorous, accessible, and actionable body of knowledge and tooling for navigating the transition to a post-labor economy — and a community capable of putting it to use.

---

**Goal:** Establish the core framework, community infrastructure, and contribution patterns that everything else builds on.

## Contribution Opportunities by Phase

If you want to contribute to current-phase work, look for `milestone: foundation` issues.

For next-phase work that isn't started yet, open a Discussion in the relevant working group thread before creating an issue — coordinate first.

---

## What's Not on the Roadmap

To keep focus, we're explicitly deferring:

- **Taking official policy positions** — we inform, we don't lobby (for now)
- **Building a membership/dues structure** — keep the barrier to entry low
- **Branching into adjacent movements** — UBI advocacy, labor organizing, etc. are related but separate; we link, we don't absorb

---

## How to Influence the Roadmap

Roadmap changes are proposals, not unilateral decisions. To propose a change:

1. Open a Discussion with the `roadmap` tag
2. Make the case: why now, what it unlocks, what it costs
3. Working group leads weigh in
4. Stewardship team incorporates into next roadmap update
