## Starting a New Working Group

See [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](../CONTRIBUTING.md#working-groups) and open a Working Group Proposal issue.

Requirements:
- Clearly defined scope
- Minimum 3 interested participants
- A proposed lead
- Differentiated from existing groups

---

## Working Group Norms

All working groups operate by the same basic norms:

- **Open attendance** at meetings — anyone can observe
- **Public meeting notes** — committed to `community/working-groups/[name]/meetings/`
- **Consensus decision-making** within the group's domain
- **Cross-group coordination** for anything that touches multiple domains
- **Working group leads** represent their group in stewardship discussions
