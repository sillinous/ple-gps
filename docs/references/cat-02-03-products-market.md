# PCF Category 2.0 — Develop and Manage Products and Services

**Domain:** Operating Process | **Value Type:** Innovation / Portfolio
**Feeds Into:** 3.0, 4.0, 5.0, 6.0
**Receives From:** 1.0, 3.1, 6.3, 6.4, 9.0, 11.0, 13.3

---

## L2: Process Groups

### 2.1 Govern and Manage Product/Service Development

#### L3 Processes
```
2.1.1  Define product/service development governance
2.1.2  Manage product/service development portfolio
2.1.3  Develop and manage product/service roadmap
2.1.4  Manage product/service development lifecycle stage gates
```

#### L4 Activities
```
2.1.1.1  Establish NPD governance structure and decision rights
2.1.1.2  Define stage-gate criteria and review cadence
2.1.2.1  Inventory all active development initiatives
2.1.2.2  Score and prioritize initiatives (strategic fit, NPV, risk)
2.1.2.3  Allocate development resources across portfolio
2.1.3.1  Map feature/capability roadmap by quarter/year
2.1.3.2  Communicate roadmap to sales, marketing, and customers
2.1.4.1  Conduct gate reviews: concept, feasibility, development, launch
2.1.4.2  Document gate decisions and rationale
```

#### Integration Triggers
- Receives ← 1.2 (strategy defines what gets funded)
- Output → 2.2, 2.3 (governs ideation and development)
- Output → 9.1 (development budget feeds financial plan)

---

### 2.2 Generate and Define New Product/Service Concepts

#### L3 Processes
```
2.2.1  Perform market and competitive research
2.2.2  Translate customer needs into product/service requirements
2.2.3  Identify new products and service innovations
2.2.4  Define and prioritize new product/service requirements
2.2.5  Evaluate and select new concepts for development
```

#### L4 Activities
```
2.2.1.1  Conduct VoC (Voice of Customer) research
2.2.1.2  Analyze competitor product gaps
2.2.1.3  Monitor emerging technology trends
2.2.2.1  Convert customer needs to functional requirements
2.2.2.2  Apply Jobs-to-be-Done framework
2.2.3.1  Run ideation workshops (internal + external)
2.2.3.2  Review patent landscape
2.2.4.1  Prioritize requirements using MoSCoW or RICE
2.2.4.2  Define MVP vs. full product scope
2.2.5.1  Score concepts against strategic criteria
2.2.5.2  Approve concepts for development stage gate
```

#### KPIs
- Concepts generated per quarter
- Concept-to-development conversion rate
- Time from insight to concept approval
- VoC coverage (% of target segments researched)

#### Integration Triggers
- Receives ← 3.1 (market research inputs)
- Receives ← 6.3 (customer service feedback creates product ideas)
- Receives ← 11.1 (risk assessment filters concepts)
- Output → 2.3 (approved concepts enter development)

```

#### L4 Activities
```
2.3.1.1  Create detailed design specifications

2.3.2.2  Conduct functional testing

2.3.3.2  Track development milestones

2.3.4.2  Run user testing sessions

# PCF Category 3.0 — Market and Sell Products and Services

## L2: Process Groups

### 3.1 Understand Markets, Customers, and Capabilities

#### L3 Processes
```
3.1.1  Perform customer and market intelligence analysis
3.1.2  Evaluate and prioritize market opportunities
3.1.3  Develop customer insights
3.1.4  Identify and target customer segments
3.1.5  Assess internal capabilities and resources
```

#### L4 Activities
```
3.1.1.1  Conduct primary market research (surveys, interviews, focus groups)
3.1.1.2  Conduct secondary market research (reports, data feeds)
3.1.1.3  Perform competitive intelligence gathering
3.1.1.4  Track market size and growth trends
3.1.2.1  Score market segments by attractiveness (size, growth, fit)
3.1.2.2  Model market entry economics
3.1.3.1  Build customer personas and journey maps
3.1.3.2  Analyze purchase decision drivers
3.1.3.3  Map customer value hierarchy
3.1.4.1  Define target segment profiles (ICP)
3.1.4.2  Estimate addressable market per segment
3.1.5.1  Map organizational capabilities to market opportunities
3.1.5.2  Identify capability gaps requiring investment or partnership
```

#### KPIs
- Market research coverage (% target segments with current data)
- Insight utilization rate (% research inputs converted to decisions)
- ICP definition completeness
- Competitive intelligence refresh cycle

#### Integration Triggers
- Output → 1.1 (market intelligence informs vision)
- Output → 2.2 (customer needs drive product concepts)
- Output → 3.2 (insights shape marketing strategy)
- Receives ← 6.3, 6.4 (customer service data as insight source)
