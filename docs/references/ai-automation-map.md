# APQC PCF — AI and Automation Opportunity Map

## Scoring System

Each process is rated on three dimensions:
- **Automation Potential (AP):** 1–5 (5 = highly automatable today)
- **AI Enhancement Potential (AEP):** 1–5 (5 = significant AI advantage)
- **ROI Priority:** High / Medium / Low

**Combined Score** = AP + AEP (max 10). Prioritize scores ≥8.

## Implementation Guidance

### Phase 1: Quick Wins (0–6 months)
Focus on AP≥4, ROI=High processes:
- AP invoice processing (9.6.1)
- Lead scoring (3.6.1)
- IT service desk (8.5.1)
- Performance dashboards (13.7.3)
- Compliance monitoring (11.2.3)

### Phase 2: Transform (6–18 months)
Focus on AEP=5, strategic impact:
- Customer service AI agents (6.3.2)
- AI forecasting across supply chain and finance
- Competitive and market intelligence (3.1.1, 1.1.2)
- Predictive maintenance (10.3.2)
- Personalized learning (7.3.2)

### Phase 3: Innovate (18+ months)
End-to-end process transformation with AI at the core:
- Autonomous demand-to-supply chains
- AI-driven continuous planning (strategy → execution)
- Predictive retention and customer success
- Fully AI-augmented knowledge organization
