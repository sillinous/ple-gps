# PCF Category 7.0 — Develop and Manage Human Capital

**Domain:** Management & Support | **Value Type:** Organizational Capability
**Feeds Into:** All operating categories (1–6) via talent supply
**Receives From:** 1.2, 3.4, 5.2, 6.2, 9.5, 13.3, 13.7

---

## L2: Process Groups

### 7.1 Develop and Manage HR Planning, Policies, and Strategies

#### L3 Processes
```
7.1.1  Develop human capital strategy

7.1.3  Monitor and update HR plans
7.1.4  Develop and manage HR policies and procedures
7.1.5  Develop and manage wellness programs
7.1.6  Ensure employee safety and security
7.1.7  Develop workforce planning and analytics
```

#### L4 Activities
```
7.1.1.1  Align HR strategy to business strategy (feeds 1.2)
7.1.1.2  Define employer value proposition (EVP)
7.1.2.1  Develop headcount plan by function/BU
7.1.2.2  Define workforce segmentation model
7.1.3.1  Review plan vs. actuals quarterly
7.1.4.1  Develop employee handbook and policy library
7.1.4.2  Manage policy review and update cycle
7.1.6.1  Develop safety policies and training
7.1.6.2  Investigate and report workplace incidents
7.1.7.1  Build workforce analytics dashboard
7.1.7.2  Analyze turnover patterns and drivers
```

#### KPIs
- Headcount plan accuracy
- Workforce analytics coverage
- Policy compliance rate
- Safety incident rate (TRIR)

### 7.2 Recruit, Source, and Select Employees

#### L3 Processes
```
7.2.1  Manage employee requisitioning
7.2.2  Recruit and source candidates
7.2.3  Screen and evaluate candidates
7.2.4  Manage candidate offers
7.2.5  Manage new hire onboarding
```

#### L4 Activities
```
7.2.1.1  Create and approve job requisitions
7.2.1.2  Confirm budget approval for headcount
7.2.2.1  Post positions (job boards, careers page, LinkedIn)
7.2.2.2  Source passive candidates
7.2.2.3  Manage employee referral program
7.2.3.1  Review resumes and screen candidates
7.2.3.2  Conduct phone/video screens
7.2.3.3  Conduct structured interviews
7.2.3.4  Assess candidates (skills testing, case studies)
7.2.3.5  Check references and conduct background checks
7.2.4.1  Develop and extend offer letter
7.2.4.2  Negotiate compensation (feeds 7.4)
7.2.4.3  Manage offer acceptance or declination
7.2.5.1  Execute pre-start paperwork and I-9
7.2.5.2  Set up IT accounts and equipment (feeds 8.5)
7.2.5.3  Complete onboarding activities (first 30/60/90 days)
7.2.5.4  Assign buddy/mentor
```

#### KPIs
- Time to fill (requisition to offer accepted)
- Time to productivity (new hire)
- Offer acceptance rate
- Quality of hire (6/12-month performance)
- Cost per hire
- Recruiter productivity (hires per recruiter)
- Source effectiveness by channel

#### Integration Triggers
- Receives ← 7.1 (workforce plan defines demand)
- Receives ← 5.2, 6.2, 4.1 (operational demand for roles)
- Output → 7.3 (new hires enter development pipeline)
- Output → 7.6 (employee records created)
- Output → 8.5 (IT needs to provision access for new hire)
- Output → 9.5 (payroll enrollment)
