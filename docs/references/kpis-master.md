# APQC PCF — Master KPI and Metrics Framework

## Framework Overview

KPIs are organized by the four APQC measurement dimensions:
- **Efficiency** — How well are resources used? (cost, time, productivity)
- **Effectiveness** — How well does the process achieve its purpose? (quality, accuracy)
- **Adaptability** — How well does the process respond to change? (cycle time, flexibility)
- **Customer Value** — How well does the process serve customers? (CSAT, NPS, resolution)

## APQC Benchmarking Notes

APQC Open Standards Benchmarking provides percentile data (25th, median, 75th, top quartile)
for most of these metrics across industries. When benchmarking:

1. Use **same industry** comparisons where possible
2. Segment by company size (revenue bands: <$1B, $1–5B, $5–25B, >$25B)
3. Focus on **25th percentile → median** as realistic near-term targets
4. Use **top quartile** as stretch/aspirational targets
5. Combine with **trend analysis** — trajectory matters as much as current position
